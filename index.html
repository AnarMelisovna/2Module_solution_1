What is a Case Competition?

Case Competition is a competition in which teams solve real business problems presented by companies. The goal is to develop analytical and strategic skills, learn how to solve practical problems, and improve teamwork.

Requirements

  1. Each team should have 3-4 students.
  2. All participants must be students of 9-12 grades.
  3. There is no need to have experience in business or economics!
